How the workflow looks

The demo video on this page is processed by the same pipeline we use for real customer content.

  1. Upload your own video (for example, an educational short or commentary clip).
  2. EchoShorts generates accurate subtitles and detects your speaking style.
  3. Our AI creates translated audio that keeps your tone and emotions, then re-syncs everything into a new language.
  4. You download the processed video and publish on your creator platforms.

Questions & compliance

Designed as a productivity tool, not a copyright service

EchoShorts focuses on empowering creators to work faster with their own content. We do not encourage copyright infringement or redistribution of protected media.

Who is EchoShorts for?

EchoShorts is built for content creators, educators, and video editors who want to localize their own videos. Typical use cases include tutorials, commentary videos, explainer content, and creator-branded shorts.

Do you provide movie or TV downloads?

No. EchoShorts is not a media download site. We do not provide movie, TV, or other copyright video downloads. Users must upload content they own or have permission to process.

No. EchoShorts is a straightforward AI video processing productivity tool. Users purchase processing credits to pay for minutes of AI translation and subtitle generation. There are no financial returns, investments, or referral commissions.
